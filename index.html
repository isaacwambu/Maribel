About Us

Maribel-Hermans Associates is an accountancy and consultancy firm licensed under the Accountants Act number 15 of 2008 offering audit & assurance, tax and business advisory services to clients in various industries.Our team serves and offer quality services across sectors in the East African region. We are dedicated in client service delivery with akin to quality and meeting customer needs and standards.

We care about

Our People

We nurture and build our teams with commitment to ethical values and providing training and capacity building to enhance expertise for quality service delivery. We endeavor to provide a working environment where every team member feels respected and valued. We cherish diversity and inclusiveness as the epitome of the firm’s values as we believe that our people and culture should reflect the diversity in our clients.

Our Clients

We listen, analyze and provide solutions to our clients’ problems while treating them with fairness, integrity and honesty.

Society

We partner with communities in projects with a view of giving back to the society. Our people are passionate and action driven in undertaking corporate social responsibility projects that uplift the less fortunate in the society.

Mission

Provide modern, innovative and sustainable audit, tax and advisory services to our esteemed clientele through knowledge sharing and mentorship.

Vision

Our Core Values

Leadership

Key Industries

Financial Services

We help corporate and family businesses through financial advisory services. The firm boasts of experienced team with expansive experience in financial industry.

Major areas of services include

  • Business Valuation
  • Due Diligence
  • Assets Management
  • Risk Management
  • Private equity Advisory

Healthcare

We partner with healthcare service providers to offer quality and affordable services. Our major service areas include:

  • Tax Advisory & Planning
  • Training Staff
  • Review of Internal Control System
  • Financial Planning & Budgeting

Energy & Extractive

Energy is the cornerstone of our economy and efficiency is critical to cost minimization. We partner with energy and extractive sector in providing:

  • Energy Audits
  • Environmental Impact Assessments
  • Financial Advisory
  • Budgeting & Planning
  • Supply Chain process & procedure reviews

Not for Profit Organisation

Maribel-Hermans is committed in providing audit & assurance, regulatory compliance and advisory services to non-governmental organisations to help them meet their strategic objectives and goals.

Retail, Wholesale & Distribution

Maribel-Hermans are passionate about the role retail, wholesale and distribution play in the economic development. We provide solutions that meet business needs. We cherish quality services offered and listen to our clients’ desire and meet their demands.

Professional Services

Through a host of technical skills within the firm, we provide various professionals with services meeting their needs. These include but not limited to:

  • Audit & assurance
  • Tax Planning
  • Payroll Services
  • Budgeting & Planning
  • Asset Valuation & Due Diligence
  • And many others.

Technology & Media

Technology drives business and acts as an enabler in the market place. We boast of wide experience in areas of information technology. We provide the following:

  • Information Systems Audit
  • Systems Review
  • Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery Services
  • Business Systems Analysis
  • ERP Systems Reviews & Installations

Public Sector

Public sector remains a critical partner with the firm. we offer the following to the public sector institutions

  • Advisory Services
  • Risk Management Training
  • Capacity Building & Development

Tourism, Hospitality & Leisure

Tourism, hospitality & leisure industry is one of the major contributors in GDP growth within the East Africa region. Maribel-Hermans is part of the success story in this industry through unique services provided to investors.

Major areas of services include:

  • Financial Planning and Analysis
  • Feasibility Studies
  • Strategic Planning & Development
  • Tax Advisory & Planning
  • Environmental, Social & Impact Assessments
  • Strategic Environmental Assessment & Planning
  • Internal Control Design & Implementation
  • Fraud Management
  • Development of Policies & Procedures

Manufacturing

Manufacturing industry contribute immensely in the GDP growth for the economy. We listen and understand the needs of manufacturing firms. We offer advisory and tax planning services and many other high quality services. Our expertise is unrivaled.

Shipping, Transport, Logistics & Maritime

Shipping, transport and logistics holds key to success to our economic development. Our economy is import driven and efficiency within the sector is key. At Maribel-Hermans, we are proud of extensive experience and knowledge within the industry. We partner with sector players through provision of the following services:

  • Tax Advisory & Planning
  • Import Advisory Services

  • Quality Management Systems
